First-Time Login

Step 1: Download App

Send client the appropriate link based on their phone.

Step 2: Open App

They'll see a login screen.

Step 3: Enter Credentials

  • Email: Their business email (from onboarding form)
  • Password: Whatever was set during setup

Step 4: Common Login Issues

IssueFix
"Invalid credentials"Reset password in GHL
"Account not found"Verify email address is correct
Forgot passwordClick "Forgot Password" in app

Password Requirements

  • Minimum 8 characters
  • At least 1 special character (! @ # $ etc.)
  • At least 1 number

When resetting: Tell client the password must include a special character.

Key Features for Clients

1. Conversations (Most Important)

What it is: Where all leads and messages appear.

How to use:

  1. Tap "Conversations" at bottom
  2. See list of all conversations
  3. Tap a conversation to open
  4. Type message and tap Send

Pro tip: Conversations show ALL channels (SMS, chat widget, email) in one place.

2. Contacts

What it is: List of all people in their system.

How to use:

  1. Tap "Contacts" at bottom
  2. Search for someone by name
  3. Tap to see their details
  4. Can call/text directly from contact card

3. Marketing - Request Review

What it is: How to send review requests.

How to use:

  1. Tap "Marketing" at bottom
  2. Tap "Forms"
  3. Select "Request Review" (or similar)
  4. Enter customer's name and phone
  5. Tap Submit
  6. Customer gets review request text automatically

When to use: Right after completing a job.

4. Notifications

How to enable:

  1. Go to phone Settings
  2. Find "Lead Connector" or "HighLevel"
  3. Enable all notifications

Tell clients: "Turn on notifications so you never miss a lead."

Common Client Questions

"Where do I see new leads?"

"Tap 'Conversations' at the bottom. All your new leads show up there. The newest ones are at the top."

"How do I text someone?"

"Tap 'Conversations', find their name, tap to open, then type your message and hit send."

"How do I call someone from the app?"

"Go to 'Contacts', find their name, tap it, then tap the phone icon."

"How do I send a review request?"

"Tap 'Marketing', then 'Forms', select 'Request Review', enter their name and phone, and submit. They'll get a text automatically."

"I'm not getting notifications"

"Go to your phone's Settings > Notifications > Lead Connector. Make sure everything is turned ON. Also check that Do Not Disturb isn't enabled."

Troubleshooting

App is slow or freezing

  1. Force close the app
  2. Reopen
  3. If still slow, try reinstalling

Messages not sending

  1. Check internet connection
  2. Check A2P status (may be pending)
  3. Try sending from desktop instead

Can't see certain features

  1. Check user permissions in GHL
  2. Some features may be admin-only

App crashes on open

  1. Update to latest app version
  2. Restart phone
  3. Reinstall app

Desktop Alternative

Clients can also access everything via browser:

  • URL: https://app.slatesystemsllc.com (or their white-label URL)
  • Same login credentials
  • Full access to all features

When to recommend desktop:

  • Client prefers computer
  • Need to do complex actions
  • App having issues

Quick Walkthrough (Loom Script)

If doing a screen-share walkthrough, cover:

  1. Login - Show where to enter credentials
  2. Conversations - Show where leads appear
  3. How to reply - Send a test message
  4. Contacts - Show the contact list
  5. Marketing form - Show how to request reviews
  6. Notifications - Show phone settings

Keep it under 5 minutes.
