How to Use This Document

  1. Client asks a question
  2. Find it in this FAQ
  3. Use the answer (customize with their name/details)
  4. If not found, escalate per escalation-rules.md

CATEGORY: WEBSITE

"How do I see my website?"

Answer:

Your website is at [their domain]. You can also find it on your quick links page at [their domain]/my-links.

"How do I update my phone number on the website?"

Answer:

Let us know the new number and we'll update it for you within 24 hours. Reply with the new number and we'll take care of it.

Note: Update custom values: company_phone__aesthetic_, company_phone__functional_, company_twilio_phone

"Can I change the text/images on my website?"

Answer:

Yes! For text changes, just tell us what you'd like changed and where. For images, send us the new images and we'll swap them out.

Note: Fulfillment handles this. Create task in pipeline.

"My website isn't loading"

Answer:

I'll check on that right now. Can you tell me what you see when you try to visit it? Also, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.

Note: Check: Domain DNS, SSL certificate, GHL site status. If actual issue, escalate to Fulfillment.

"The chat widget isn't working"

Answer:

Let me check on that. Can you tell me what happens when you try to use it? I'll look into it right now.

Note: Check: Chat widget enabled on site, correct configuration.

CATEGORY: APP / LEAD CONNECTOR

"How do I download the app?"

Answer:

Here are the links:
- iPhone: https://apps.apple.com/app/highlevel/id1459516266
- Android: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.gohighlevel

After downloading, use your email and the password we set up. Let me know if you need help logging in.

"I can't log in to the app"

Answer:

No problem! I'll reset your password right now. Make sure the new password has at least one special character (like ! or @). I'll text you the new password in a moment.

Note: Reset in GHL: Sub-account > Team > User > Reset password

"Where do I see my leads?"

Answer:

In the app, tap "Conversations" at the bottom. All your leads show up there. You can also tap "Contacts" to see everyone in your system.

"How do I respond to leads?"

Answer:

In the app, tap "Conversations", then tap on the lead's name. You can text them right from there. Just type your message and hit send.

"I'm not getting notifications"

Answer:

Let's fix that. Go to your phone's Settings > Notifications > Lead Connector (or HighLevel). Make sure notifications are turned ON. Also check that "Do Not Disturb" isn't enabled.

CATEGORY: REVIEWS

"How do I get more reviews?"

Answer:

Use the marketing form in your app! Go to Marketing > Forms > "Request Review". Enter your customer's name and phone, hit send, and they'll get a link to leave a review. We recommend doing this right after you finish a job.

"Where do I find my review link?"

Answer:

Your Google review link is on your quick links page: [their domain]/my-links. You can also copy it and text it directly to customers.

"How does the review system work?"

Answer:

When a customer clicks your review link:
- If they click 4-5 stars, they go straight to Google to leave a review
- If they click 1-3 stars, they go to a private feedback form

This protects your Google rating while still getting feedback from unhappy customers.

"Can I respond to Google reviews?"

Answer:

Yes! Our system automatically responds to positive reviews (4-5 stars) for you. If you want to respond to one personally, you can do that in your Google Business Profile.

"I got a negative review"

Answer:

Sorry to hear that. If it's from a real customer, the best approach is to respond professionally and try to make it right. Would you like some help crafting a response?

Note: If fake review, guide them on flagging it in Google.

CATEGORY: PHONE / SMS

"My phone number isn't working"

Answer:

Let me check on that. Can you tell me what's happening? Are calls not coming through, or is it texts?

Note: Check: Phone forwarding config, A2P status, number not suspended

"Texts aren't sending"

Answer:

This could be related to your A2P verification. Let me check the status. It usually takes 5-7 business days to get approved after we submit.

Note: Check A2P status. If rejected, see a2p-submission.md

"What's A2P?"

Answer:

A2P stands for Application-to-Person. It's a verification process required by phone carriers to send automated texts. We submit this for you, and it usually takes 5-7 business days to get approved.

"I'm getting spam calls"

Answer:

Unfortunately, spam calls are an industry-wide issue. Your toll-free number is new, so it may get fewer spam calls than your personal number. You can also block specific numbers in the app.

Response Tone Guidelines

DO:

  • Use their first name
  • Be friendly and helpful
  • Keep it short and simple
  • Offer solutions, not excuses
  • Follow up to confirm resolution

DON'T:

  • Use jargon (CRM, pipeline, etc.)
  • Be defensive
  • Over-explain
  • Make promises you can't keep
  • Leave issues unresolved
